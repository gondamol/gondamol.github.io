# Nic | Data

Quarto + Quarto-rendered portfolio for Nichodemus Amollo — research data management, analytics engineering, and AI/system design from Nairobi, Kenya.

## What you find here

- A homepage that frames field experience, analytics systems, and the AI transition in one narrative.
- About, CV, and skills pages that name the operational delivery, training, and architectural work tying governance, health financing, and ML together.
- Featured projects (ML systems, ETL pipelines, LLM briefs, finance case study, dashboard concepts) grouped in `projects/`.
- Research, blog, and life writing that documents the Kisumu NCD work, the analytic playbooks, and the personal story backing the practice.

## Portfolio highlights

- **Digital-first research data work**: Surveys, dashboards, and governance labs in `projects/analytics-engineering/` and `projects/data-engineering/`.
- **ML-infrastructure narrative**: Healthcare readmission, ML monitoring, and predictive dashboards all live alongside the new blog series on reproducible ML, domain knowledge, and AI for low-resource settings.
- **Evidence translation**: Case studies, CV, and skills sections all link back to active engagements with Georgetown GUI2DE, KEMRI, and regional partners.

Salary Negotiation

Research First:

  • Levels.fyi - Tech compensation
  • Glassdoor - Company-specific
  • Payscale - General salaries
  • H1B Data - What companies pay (public data)

Know Your Worth:

Calculate your market value: 1. Base: Your experience + location 2. Add: Industry premium (+10-30%) 3. Add: Company size premium (+0-40%) 4. Add: Hot skills (Python, ML) (+5-15%)

Negotiation Script:

When they ask your salary expectations:

"I'm looking for total compensation in the range of [X-Y] based 
on my research for this role in [location] with my experience 
level. However, I'm flexible and would love to hear what you 
have budgeted for this position."

When you get an offer:

"Thank you for the offer! I'm excited about the role. I was 
expecting something closer to [Y] based on [specific reason: 
market rate, my experience, competing offer]. Is there 
flexibility in the base salary or total compensation package?"

If they say no:

"I understand. Are there other aspects of the offer we could 
discuss? Such as:
- Signing bonus
- Additional PTO
- Remote work flexibility
- Professional development budget
- Earlier performance review"

Salary Growth Timeline

Year 0-2: Learning Phase - Starting: $55-65K - After 1 year: $60-75K - Growth: ~10-15% with job switch

Year 2-5: Growth Phase - Starting Year 2: $75-85K - After Year 5: $95-110K - Growth: ~15-20% per job switch

Year 5-8: Senior Phase - Starting Year 5: $110-120K - After Year 8: $130-145K - Growth: ~10-15% per job switch

Year 8+: Leadership Phase - Manager track: $145-200K+ - IC track: $145-180K

How to Increase Your Salary

Strategy 1: Job Switch (Fastest)

Reality: Staying at one company = 3-5% raises
Job switching: 15-30% salary jumps

Optimal frequency: Every 2-3 years

Strategy 2: Learn High-Value Skills

Skills that pay more (+10-20%): - Machine learning - Cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP) - Big data (Spark, Hadoop) - Programming (Python > SQL only) - Advanced statistics

Strategy 3: Target High-Paying Industries

Career path example: 1. Year 0: Retail - $60K 2. Year 2: Healthcare - $80K 3. Year 5: Tech - $115K 4. Year 8: FAANG - $160K+

Strategy 4: Get Promoted

Timeline at typical company: - Analyst ‚Üí Senior Analyst: 2-3 years (+20-30%) - Senior Analyst ‚Üí Lead: 3-4 years (+25-35%) - Lead ‚Üí Manager: 2-3 years (+20-30%)

RED FLAGS in Job Offers

Avoid if: - ‚ùå Salary 20%+ below market - ‚ùå ‚ÄúWe pay in experience/exposure‚Äù - ‚ùå Unclear bonus/commission structure - ‚ùå Excessive unpaid overtime expected - ‚ùå No clear career growth path

Green flags: - ‚úÖ Transparent salary range in posting - ‚úÖ Clear leveling system - ‚úÖ Annual raises + bonuses - ‚úÖ Professional development budget - ‚úÖ Equity/stock options

Beyond Base Salary

Total compensation includes: - Base salary (60-80% of total) - Annual bonus (5-30%) - Stock/equity (0-40% at startups/tech) - Benefits (health, dental, vision) - 401(k) match (3-6%) - PTO (2-6 weeks) - Remote work (worth $5-15K)

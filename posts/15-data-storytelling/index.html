## The 3-Act Structure for Data Stories

### **Act 1: The Setup (Context)**
- What's the current situation?
- Why does it matter?
- What question are we answering?

### **Act 2: The Conflict (Insight)**
- What did the data reveal?
- What's surprising or important?
- Show the evidence

### **Act 3: The Resolution (Action)**
- What should we do?
- What's the expected impact?
- What's the next step?

## The SCQA Framework

**Situation:** Current state  
**Complication:** The problem  
**Question:** What should we do?  
**Answer:** Your recommendation

**Example:**
- **S:** "We spend $100K monthly on Facebook ads"
- **C:** "But 70% of leads don't convert"
- **Q:** "How do we improve lead quality?"

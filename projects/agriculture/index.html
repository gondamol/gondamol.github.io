Agriculture & Agribusiness Analytics

Data-driven investment analysis for farming ventures in Homabay County, Kenya

🌾 Overview

Applying data science and economic analysis to agricultural investment decisions in Homabay County, Kenya. These projects combine statistical analysis, financial modeling, and local agricultural knowledge to support evidence-based farming ventures.

Personal Context

I’m planning commercial farming operations in Rachuonyo North (Homa Hills), Suba, and Ndhiwa sub-counties in Homabay County - my place of birth. These analyses inform real investment decisions for livestock and crop farming.

Current Operations: 7 goats + 15 sheep | Target: Commercial scale by 2026

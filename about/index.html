About Me

Senior Research Data Manager | Health Data Analytics Specialist

LinkedIn Github Twitter Kaggle Email

Who I Am

I am a Senior Research Data Manager with over 7 years of specialized experience in health data analytics, survey design, and comprehensive data management. My career has been dedicated to transforming complex health data into actionable insights that drive evidence-based decision-making in resource-limited settings.

My expertise spans the complete data lifecycle—from designing robust data collection systems using SurveyCTO, REDCap, and KoboCollect to performing advanced statistical analysis with R, Python, Stata, and SQL. I have managed data for 20+ multi-site research projects, including clinical trials, cohort studies, and large-scale health surveys, consistently achieving 99%+ data quality.

My Journey

My path into health data science emerged from a deep conviction that rigorous data management is the foundation of credible research. Over the past 7+ years, I’ve had the privilege of:

  • Leading data management for multi-country HIV treatment trials with 1,200+ participants
  • Designing electronic data capture systems for studies enrolling 5,000+ participants
  • Creating interactive dashboards that reduce decision-making time from weeks to minutes
  • Training 500+ researchers in R programming and data management best practices
  • Contributing to health policy decisions through evidence-based analytics

What Drives Me

🎯 My Professional Mission

To bridge the gap between raw health data and meaningful insights that improve lives. I believe that:

  • Data quality is non-negotiable - Every data point represents a person
  • Open science advances health - Reproducible research should be the standard
  • Technology should empower - Tools should solve problems, not create them
  • Capacity building multiplies impact - Teaching others creates lasting change

Current Focus

🎓 MSc Thesis Research

I’m currently completing my MSc in Epidemiology and Biostatistics at JOOUST, with my thesis examining:

“Exploring Financial Determinants of Effective Hypertension and Diabetes Management in Primary Health Facilities in Kisumu County”

This research investigates the critical intersection of health facility financing and NCD management outcomes, employing: - Mixed-methods case study design - Financial assessments of 30+ health facilities - Patient outcome tracking (500+ participants) - Cost-effectiveness analysis - Interactive dashboards for real-time monitoring
